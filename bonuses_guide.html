Wagering Requirements Explained

The most important aspect of any bonus is the wagering requirement (also called "playthrough" or "rollover"). This is how many times you must bet the bonus amount (or bonus + deposit) before you can withdraw winnings.

Important Terms & Restrictions

üö´ Common Bonus Restrictions

Maximum Bet Limit

While using a bonus, you typically cannot bet more than $5 per spin/hand. Exceeding this can void your bonus and winnings.

Time Limits

Most bonuses must be used within 7-30 days or they expire, along with any associated winnings.

Maximum Cashout

Bonuses often cap how much you can withdraw. Example: $50 no-deposit bonus might have $100 max cashout.

Game Restrictions

Certain games may be completely excluded from bonus play. Check the terms before playing.

Minimum Deposit

Deposit match bonuses usually require a minimum deposit (e.g., $20) to qualify.

Country Restrictions

Some bonuses aren't available in all countries. Check eligibility before claiming.

One Bonus Per Household

Most welcome bonuses are limited to one per person/household/IP address.

Smart Bonus Strategies

üí° Calculate Realistic Completion

Before claiming, calculate if you can realistically meet the requirements within your budget. Multiply the wagering requirement by expected loss per bet (based on house edge) to estimate cost.

üí° Stick to High RTP Games

When clearing wagering requirements, play games with the highest RTP (96%+) that contribute 100%. This maximizes your chances of completing requirements with your bonus intact.

üí° Read Everything Twice

Bonus terms can be complex. Read all terms and conditions carefully before accepting. If anything is unclear, contact customer support for clarification.

üí° Track Your Progress

Monitor how much wagering you've completed. Most casinos show this in your account, but keep your own records to stay on top of requirements.

üí° Time Your Deposits

If a bonus has a time limit, consider when you'll actually have time to play. Don't claim a 7-day bonus if you're going on vacation for a week.

üí° Compare Multiple Casinos

Different casinos offer vastly different bonus terms. Shop around and compare wagering requirements, game contributions, and restrictions before choosing.

Bonus Abuse & Fair Play

‚ö†Ô∏è Activities That Can Get You Banned

  • Multiple Accounts: Creating multiple accounts to claim the same bonus repeatedly
  • Opposite Betting: Betting both red and black in roulette to meet requirements risk-free
  • Low-Risk Strategies: Using certain betting patterns designed solely to clear wagering
  • Exceeding Bet Limits: Betting more than allowed while bonus is active
  • Bonus Hunting Networks: Coordinating with others to exploit bonuses
  • False Information: Providing fake details to claim bonuses

Casinos actively monitor for bonus abuse and will confiscate winnings and ban accounts that violate terms. Always play fairly and within the rules.

When to Decline a Bonus

Sometimes the best decision is to not accept a bonus. Consider declining if:

  • Wagering requirements are excessively high (60x+)
  • Your preferred games don't contribute or contribute very little
  • You want the freedom to withdraw at any time
  • Bet restrictions are too limiting for your play style
  • Time pressure would force you to play beyond your budget
  • You simply want to play without restrictions

Remember: Many casinos allow you to opt out of bonuses. If you prefer unrestricted play, you can often contact support to have your account flagged to not receive automatic bonuses.
