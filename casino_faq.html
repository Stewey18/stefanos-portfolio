Bonuses & Promotions

How do I claim the welcome bonus? +
Claiming your welcome bonus is automatic:

  2. Make your first qualifying deposit (usually $10 minimum)
  3. The bonus is automatically credited to your account

What are wagering requirements? +
Wagering requirements (also called playthrough) determine how much you must bet before you can withdraw bonus winnings.

Example: You receive a $100 bonus with 30x wagering requirements.

  • Required playthrough: $100 √ó 30 = $3,000
  • You must wager $3,000 in total before withdrawing
  • Different games contribute different percentages toward requirements

Game Contributions:

  • Slots: Usually 100%
  • Video Poker: 10-20%
  • Blackjack: 10-20%
  • Roulette: 10-20%
  • Live Dealer: 0-10%
Always read the full bonus terms. Wagering requirements must typically be completed within 7-30 days.
Can I withdraw my bonus? +
The bonus itself cannot be withdrawn. However:
  • You can withdraw winnings earned using the bonus
  • You must complete wagering requirements first
  • Some bonuses are "sticky" (removed upon withdrawal)
  • Some bonuses are "non-sticky" (can be cancelled early)

Example: You deposit $100 and receive a $100 bonus. You win $500. After completing wagering requirements, you can withdraw your $100 deposit + $500 winnings. The $100 bonus is removed.

Why was my bonus cancelled? +
Bonuses can be cancelled for several reasons:
  • Wagering deadline expired: You didn't complete requirements in time
  • Terms violated: You exceeded maximum bet limits or played restricted games
  • You requested withdrawal: Some bonuses are forfeited when you withdraw
  • Multiple accounts: Bonus abuse detected
  • You cancelled it: You can manually cancel bonuses in some cases
Bonus Terms: Always carefully read the terms and conditions for each bonus. Pay special attention to max bet rules, game restrictions, and time limits.

What does RTP mean? +
RTP stands for "Return to Player" ‚Äì it's the percentage of all wagered money that a game will pay back to players over time.

Example: A slot with 96% RTP will theoretically return $96 for every $100 wagered.

Important points:

  • RTP is calculated over millions of spins
  • It doesn't guarantee individual session results
  • Higher RTP = better long-term player odds
  • RTP doesn't indicate hit frequency or volatility

Typical RTP ranges:

  • Slots: 94-98%
  • Blackjack (optimal play): 99.5%
  • Roulette: 94.7-97.3%
  • Baccarat: 98.9%

How do I self-exclude? +

Permanent exclusion: Minimum 1 year (typically permanent)
