Connection & Streaming Issues

Video is freezing or buffering

Try these steps in order:

  1. Check your internet speed (we recommend at least 2 Mbps)
  2. Close other apps or browser tabs using bandwidth
  3. Switch to lower quality video in settings menu
  4. Try Main View (uses less bandwidth than close-up views)
  5. Restart the game if issues persist
